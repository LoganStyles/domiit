how it works

questions & answers

Ask questions so that other users on the platform could answer or answer other asker's questions too. You can also ask and post questions directly based on request for more detailed answers to domiit experts whom are selected users approved by the platform.

articles & reviews

Write or post articles directly on the platform or upload/attach documents from external sources for other users to carry out reviews. You can also review other user’s articles on the platform or make requests for reviews exclusively from domiit experts.

riddles & solutions

Post riddles for others to provide solutions for them or write solutions for other user’s posted riddles. You can also make requestsfor solutions directly from domiit experts for enhanced solutions.

posted books

Authors and book vendors can post the cover pages of their books and other publications directly on the platform for advertisement at a premium.

requests

Users can exclusively send their requests to experts for direct answers, reviews, and solutions through the following sub-menus;Request Answer (RA), Request Review (RR), and Request Solution (RS).The experts will respond to the requests based on their preferences.

domiit experts

A domiit expert is an individual on the platform that have been verified as an excellent intellectual contributor in the areas of answering questions, reviewing articles and proffering solutions for riddles. Such individual will be certified and recognised as an experts by the platform and would be tagged with letters ‘DE’.

additional features

advertisement

Apart from the normal advert from publishers for both desktop and mobile version; this platform will enable self-serve advertisement through its self-serve ad manager.

download

Users can download materials from articles posted on the platform at a freemium or upgrade to a premium account to download more.

data storage spaces

There is a data storage space for users in their ‘my docs’ document space. Users can store documents up to 500MB disc space on a freemium account. However, they can upgrade their storage spaces up to 20GB disc space on a premium account.

social interconnectivity

Enabling effective and seamless interconnectivity between people of like minds, diverse ideas and opinions is our main goal. Therefore, this knowledge interconnectivity platform will allow users to enjoy varied and dynamic experiences in intellectual content sharing.

instant cross referencing

Cross referencing of various knowledge tools and resources is much easier if all are integrated into a single platform. And that is exactly what this concept is all about; bringing the entire resources into one fold to enable easy cross referencing to maximised its knowledge centric output.

personalised online library

Allowing users to download contents for their personal use will enable personalised online library handy and interesting. Since the platform provides a storage system for downloadable materials, users will explore this opportunity to download as much as they can for their future use.

content sorting

Sorting out the best answers, reviews and solutions based on the number of up votes and views from other users will enable a unique prioritization of contents to add more value. Therefore at a click of a button, the platform will sort out the best responses based on the highest number of up votes or views from other users.
